Our Spacifications

Classroom Instruction

Most driving school programs begin with classroom instruction. In these sessions, you will learn about traffic laws, road signs, defensive driving techniques, and other essential information related to driving. The classroom sessions are typically conducted by experienced instructors who will provide you with the necessary knowledge to become a safe and responsible driver.

Practical Driving Lessons:

After completing the classroom instruction, you will progress to practical driving lessons. These lessons are conducted in a dual-controlled vehicle with a qualified driving instructor. During the practical lessons, you will learn various driving skills such as starting, stopping, turning, lane changing, parking, and merging into traffic. The instructor will guide you through each step and provide feedback on your driving performance.

Simulation Training:

Some driving schools offer simulation training, which allows you to practice driving scenarios in a virtual environment. This can be beneficial for learners to gain experience in different road conditions, adverse weather, and challenging situations without the risks associated with real-life driving.

Defensive Driving Techniques:

Defensive Driving Techniques: A significant focus of driving school programs is teaching defensive driving techniques. These techniques emphasize awareness, anticipation, and proactive measures to prevent accidents and respond effectively to potential hazards on the road.

Rules of the Road:

Driving school programs cover the local traffic laws and regulations specific to your area. This includes information about speed limits, right-of-way rules, parking regulations, and other legal requirements that drivers must follow.

Practice Tests:

To prepare you for the written driving test required to obtain a driver's license, driving school programs often include practice tests. These tests help you familiarize yourself with the format of the actual exam and assess your knowledge of traffic rules and road signs.
